From Childhood Passion: My Billiard Obsession Began Here.

My love for billiards began in early childhood, and over the years, I have achieved significant results that have allowed me to stand out in this sport. Among my greatest achievements are finishing ninth in the national championship for juniors under 19, several times achieving ninth place in the national league, and notably, one fifth place. Furthermore, I managed to qualify for the Masters of the top 64 players from Bosnia and Herzegovina, which represents one of my most significant accomplishments to date.

SBK Dynamic: Where Champions Break Through in Sarajevo.

I was born in Sarajevo and am proud to be a member of the SBK Dynamic club, which has been a great support system on my path in billiards. Additionally, I am a second-year IT student, which has taught me the importance of strategy, focus, and continuous learning – skills that are equally important on the billiard table.

My Unexpected Path to Semi-Pro Pool.

It all started with casual games at the local pub, really. Just me, trying to sink a few balls on a beat-up table, with friends cheering or laughing along. Somehow, that chill vibe and the clinks of pool balls got under my skin. Before I knew it, what was just a fun way to kill time turned into something I got pretty good at—like, semi-pro good. Funny how things turn out, isn't it?
