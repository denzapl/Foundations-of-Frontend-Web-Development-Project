Resilience on the Felt

Hello! I’m Daniel Planinčić, a 20-year-old billiards player from Sarajevo with a story to share. My billiards journey kicked off in childhood, leading to notable achievements like a ninth place in the under-19 national championship, several top-ten finishes in the national league, and making it to the top 64 players in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Besides billiards, I'm also an IT student at Burch University, where strategy and focus intertwine with my sport.

Life's thrown challenges my way, including a kindey transplant, but with my family's support—especially my superhero dad—I've never stopped pushing forward. Billiards is more than a game to me; it's about resilience, passion, and inspiring others, no matter the obstacles.

Thanks for diving into my story.


Daniel Planinčić
